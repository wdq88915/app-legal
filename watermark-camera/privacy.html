Privacy Policy

WatermarkCamera

Last Updated: January 2026

WatermarkCamera ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the basic features of WatermarkCamera. The app is designed to function entirely on your device.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality and crash reporting purposes only.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics may be collected to improve app performance and user experience.

1.3 Information Accessed with Your Permission

  • Camera: Required to capture photos and videos. Images are processed locally on your device.
  • Photo Library: Required to save edited photos/videos and import existing media. We do not upload your photos to any server.
  • Location: Optional, used only when you choose to add location-based features. Location data is processed locally and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Microphone: Required only for video recording with audio.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our application
  • Process your photos and videos locally on your device
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

Important: All photo and video processing occurs entirely on your device. We do not have access to your photos, videos, or personal media files.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Processing

All image processing and media editing are performed locally on your device. Your photos and videos never leave your device unless you choose to share them.

3.2 No Cloud Storage

We do not store your photos, videos, or personal data on any cloud servers. Your media remains on your device and under your control.

3.3 Subscription Data

If you subscribe to our premium features, your subscription is managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Apple Services

  • App Store: Handles all in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • StoreKit: Manages subscription verification
  • WeatherKit: May provide weather data (uses your approximate location)
  • MapKit: May provide map data for location features

4.2 Analytics

We may use Apple's built-in analytics to collect anonymous crash reports and usage statistics to improve app stability and performance.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may disclose information:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect and defend our rights or property
  • To prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing
  • To protect the personal safety of users or the public

6. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

  • You can revoke camera, photo library, location, or microphone permissions at any time through your device settings
  • You can delete the app and all associated data at any time
  • You can request deletion of any data we may have by contacting us

7.2 Location Services

Location features are entirely optional. You can use the app without enabling location services.

8. International Data Transfers

As all data processing occurs locally on your device, no international data transfers of your personal media occur. Any anonymous analytics data may be processed in various locations where Apple operates its services.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of the application after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we do not collect, store, or sell personal information, these provisions have limited applicability to our app.

11. GDPR Compliance (For EU Users)

For users in the European Union:

  • We process minimal data and only for legitimate purposes
  • All processing occurs locally on your device
  • You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data
  • You have the right to data portability
  • You may lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
