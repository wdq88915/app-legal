Privacy Policy

For the iOS app: Watermark Camera

Last updated: January 3, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Watermark Camera (the “App”) handles information when you use it.

2. Information We Collect

The App is designed to work primarily on-device. We do not require you to create an account.

  • No personal data collection: We do not collect, store, or sell personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.
  • Photos and videos: Photos and videos you capture or select are processed on your device to apply watermarks and export results. We do not upload your media to our servers.

3. Permissions

The App may request the following iOS permissions. You can control these at any time in Settings → Privacy.

  • Camera: to capture photos or videos inside the App.
  • Photos: to select existing media and/or save watermarked exports to your photo library.
  • Microphone (optional): only if you record video with sound.

4. Sharing

The App does not share your personal information with third parties. If you choose to share an exported image/video using the iOS share sheet, the content is shared to the destination you select and is then governed by that provider’s policies.

5. Third-Party Services

At the time of writing, the App does not include third-party advertising SDKs and does not use third-party analytics to track you across apps or websites.

6. Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal data on our servers, we do not retain such data. Media you create or save is stored on your device and in your photo library (if you choose to save it).

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and update the “Last updated” date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@yourdomain.com.
